# Entity Separation — Pathways VolunteerConnect

## Legal Ownership

Pathways VolunteerConnect is software intellectual property owned exclusively by **REJG Legacy Labs LLC**.

HOH Foundation accesses this platform under an Intercompany Services Agreement (MSA) and has **no ownership claim** to the code, schema, brand, or IP.

## Why This Structure Exists

| Reason | Explanation |
|---|---|
| SaaS revenue | REJG Legacy Labs can license to external nonprofits without HOH involvement |
| 501(c)(3) compliance | HOH Foundation does not own for-profit software IP |
| IRS private benefit | HOH pays fair market rate via MSA — clean arm's-length transaction |
| Scalability | Platform grows independently of HOH operational budget |
